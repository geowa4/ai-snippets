One suggestion was made by a memberta-theMembers' Relations Committee. That suggestion was to do with the auction. The point was made that all sales of equipment, etc. that are within the set price limitations of $75.00 tops and $2.00 minimum, be made through the auctioneer only. The Board of Directors agrees with that suggestion but we must recognize that it is a difficult matter to control. There are always a few cases where a member brings in an item with his price affixed and he is approached by another member offering to buy it. Rather then wait until the item is brought up for auction and the risk of losing the sale, the equipment is sold on the spot. We will make every effort to avoid private sales but we can't -romise 100% compliance.
